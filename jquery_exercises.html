My MCU Opinions

I just saw Black Widow and I want to share my thoughts on MCU movies once again.

    My top 5 MCU films are as follows(in no particular order because I don't want to have that internal debate right now):
  • Thor: Ragnarok
  • Spider-Man: Far From Home
  • Guardians of the Galaxy
  • Captain America: Civil War
  • Avengers: Infinity War

Now I know that not having Endgame in my top 5 may be controversial, but coming from someone who saw it 3 different times in theatres, the first 2 hours can lag a little after you've seen it once.
